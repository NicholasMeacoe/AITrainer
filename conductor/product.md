# Product Guide: AITrainer

## Overview
AITrainer is a web-based educational platform designed to empower individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence. The platform provides structured, persona-based learning paths, focusing on both technical mastery and strategic leadership.

## Target Audience
- **Technical Learners:** Developers, data scientists, and engineers looking to deepen their practical skills in AI tooling, APIs, and implementation.
- **Strategic Leaders:** Business executives, managers, and product owners seeking to understand AI's strategic implications, governance, and ethical considerations.
- **AI Trainers:** Individuals who are training others in AI.

## Core Features (MVP)
- **Persona-Based Learning Paths:** Tailored educational journeys that adapt to the user's role and existing knowledge level.
- **Interactive Lessons:** Engaging content including quizzes, coding labs (where applicable), and progress tracking to reinforce learning.
- **AI Sandbox:** A secure, built-in environment for learners to experiment with AI models, tools, and APIs without setting up local infrastructure.
- **Progress Tracking:** A centralized dashboard for users to monitor their advancement through various modules and paths.
- **Multi-Persona Interface:** A dynamic interface that adjusts content and complexity based on the active user persona.

## Primary Platform
- **Web Application:** A responsive and accessible web-based experience, ensuring learners can access content across various devices.

## Educational Focus
- **Tooling & APIs:** Practical, hands-on guidance on using LLMs, diffusers, and other cutting-edge AI tools.
- **AI Concepts:** A foundational understanding of AI concepts and their practical applications.
- **End-to-End Curriculum:** A comprehensive range of topics, from foundational AI principles to advanced engineering and strategic deployment.
