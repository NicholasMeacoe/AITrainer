# AITrainer

Empowering individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to navigate the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence.

## 🚀 Overview

**AITrainer** is a web-based educational platform designed to provide structured, persona-based learning paths for AI. Whether you are a developer looking to master AI tooling, a strategic leader understanding the business implications of AI, or an AI trainer teaching others, AITrainer offers a tailored experience to meet your goals.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Persona-Based Learning Paths:** Educational journeys that adapt to your specific role and knowledge level.
- **Interactive Lessons:** Engaging content including quizzes, coding labs, and progress tracking.
- **AI Sandbox:** A secure, built-in environment to experiment with AI models, tools, and APIs without local setup.
- **Multi-Persona Interface:** A dynamic UI that adjusts content and complexity based on the active user persona.
- **End-to-End Curriculum:** Covering everything from foundational AI principles to advanced engineering and strategic deployment.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

### Frontend
- **React (TypeScript):** Robust, interactive, and type-safe UI.
- **Vanilla CSS:** Flexible and precise styling for a vibrant visual aesthetic.
- **Zustand/React Context:** Efficient application state management.

### Backend
- **Python (FastAPI):** High-performance, asynchronous backend ideal for AI/ML integrations.
- **PostgreSQL:** Relational database for user profiles, progress, and structured content.
- **Vector Database (Pinecone/PGVector):** Efficient semantic search and RAG capabilities.

### AI & Machine Learning
- **AI Platform APIs:** Integration with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini.
- **Vector Search/RAG:** Context-aware answers and intelligent curriculum navigation.

### Infrastructure
- **Docker:** Containerization for consistent development and production environments.
- **GitHub Actions:** Automated CI/CD pipelines.
